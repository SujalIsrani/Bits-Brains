NeuroType
Typing Biomarkers for Early Parkinson’s Detection

NeuroType is an AI-powered typing test designed to detect subtle motor signs associated with Parkinson’s Disease by analyzing how you type.

In just 5 minutes, by typing a simple paragraph, NeuroType captures your typing rhythm, speed, and keypress patterns to generate an early assessment of Parkinsonian signs—non-invasively and remotely.

This tool is intended as an assistive screening aid, not a diagnosis. Your privacy is respected at all times.
